ERM - Emotion Recognition for Music Recommendation

ERM

Emotion Recognition for Music Recommendation

ERM (Emotion Recognition for Music Recommendation) is an intelligent platforme capable of extracting emotions' features from people's brain by using a special
electronic elemet, made of 5 electrodes.

Thanks to this incredible new technology, an ad hoc Music Recommender System is able to suggest the best song choise for the user, related to his/her emotional status.

ERM is a project in collaboration between Prof. Carmelo Ardito and Prof. Tommaso Colafiglio and the ERM students teams, composed by:
